Honolulu easily tops people’s favorite places to travel due to its uniqueness. There are so many different activities to do there it making it hard to have a dull time. The landscape of the island and features it contains also make for an amazing experience. Some of the major attractions of Honolulu include Pearl Harbor, Hanauma Bay, and Diamond head.

Prayitno, "Waikiki, Honolulu, Hawaii," 2014

Diamond Head State Monument is currently an inactive volcano that makes an amazing addition to your trip. It was originally used as a post for the US army to prevent attacks against Honolulu, but it’s now and amazing tourist attraction. This crater, known as Diamond Head, is over 300,000 years old and allows for a breathtaking one and a half to two hour hike.

Imperial Japanese Navy, Official U.S. Navy photograph, "Photograph taken from a Japanese plane during the torpedo attack on ships moored on both sides of Ford Island shortly after the beginning of the Pearl Harbor attack," 7 December 1941

Honolulu also contains Pearl Harbor, an important part of American history. Visiting Pearl Harbor can be the experience of a lifetime and enlighten you on a major event in history. Although Pearl Harbor remains an active military base, it is filled with museums and attractions that strive to teach you about the events that occurred there. Visiting Pearl Harbor also allows you to honor the fallen soldiers who lost their lives in the battle.

Prayitno, Hanauma Bay,Outside Honolulu, Hawaii, 25 September 2014

Hanauma Bay is another breathtaking attraction of Hawaii that you can’t miss out on. The crystal clear waters seem like something out of a storybook. The water’s temperature is always temperate so you don’t have to worry about getting too cold while swimming. The calm ocean also provides for a relaxing and danger-free experience. Visiting Hanauma Bay will, most definitely, make for an experience you will never forget.
